Edit Region: Custom Circle

This region is a circle whose radius is a specified number of kilometers. The center of the circle is located at a specified latitude and longitude.

 

Radius

The region's radius, in kilometers.

The maximum allowed radius is 20,000 km, which is slightly less than 180 degrees of arc.

 

Center Latitude
Center Longitude

These fields contain the latitude and longitude of the region's center, in decimal degrees.

The latitude can range from -90.0 to 90.0, with positive values denoting the northern hemisphere.

The longitude can range from -180.0 to 180.0, with positive values denoting the eastern hemisphere.

 

Min Depth
Max Depth

These fields contain the minimum and maximum allowed depth of aftershocks and foreshocks, in kilometers. Any aftershocks or foreshocks whose depth lies outside this range are ignored.

The default values are -5.0 and 700.0, which is the largest depth range allowed by OpenSHA. It is strongly recommended that you not change these value.
